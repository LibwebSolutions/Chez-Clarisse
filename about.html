Our Story

Chez Clarisse Mama Africa

Nestled in the vibrant heart of Osu, Accra, Chez Clarisse Mama Africa isn't just a restaurant — it's an unforgettable cultural journey. Since its humble beginnings, Chez Clarisse has captured the spirit of West African culinary traditions with a twist of Ivorian authenticity and Ghanaian warmth.

Renowned for its sizzling grilled tilapia, attiéké, jollof, and aromatic sauces, the restaurant embodies hospitality and home-cooked love. Whether you're relaxing under the stars or savoring a spicy meal at lunch, the vibrant ambiance and friendly staff make every visit a celebration.

Founded by the passionate Clarisse herself, the restaurant honors her vision of food that brings people together, connects cultures, and leaves you smiling. Today, it's a must-visit for food lovers, tourists, locals, and anyone craving soul-satisfying African cuisine.
